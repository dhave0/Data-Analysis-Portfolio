# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## 🔗Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard developed in Microsoft Power BI to help business stakeholders monitor sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights using interactive visuals, KPIs, and filters.

---

## 📌Business Problem

Sales data often exists in multiple tables and formats, making it difficult for management to quickly answer questions such as:

- How much revenue are we generating?
- Which products generate the highest sales?
- Which regions perform best?
- Are profits increasing over time?
- Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a centralized, interactive reporting solution.

---

## 📌Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Track total profit
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare regional sales
- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Understand customer purchasing behavior

---

## 🎬Dashboard Features

### 📌Executive KPIs

The dashboard displays:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin

These KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.

---

### Sales Trend Analysis

A time-series visualization shows sales performance over time, helping identify:

- Growth periods
- Seasonal patterns
- Sales declines

---

### 📌Regional Performance

A map/chart compares sales across different regions, making it easier to identify:

- High-performing markets
- Underperforming regions
- Geographic opportunities

---

### 📌Product Performance

The dashboard ranks products based on:

- Sales
- Revenue contribution
- Profit

This enables management to focus on best-selling products.

---

### 📌Category Analysis

Products are grouped into categories to compare:

- Revenue
- Profitability
- Overall contribution

---

### Customer Insights

The dashboard also provides customer-level analysis, showing which customers generate the highest revenue.

---

## 📌Business Insights

From the dashboard, decision-makers can:

- Identify top-performing regions
- Monitor monthly sales growth
- Detect low-performing products
- Prioritize profitable product categories
- Focus marketing efforts on high-value customers
